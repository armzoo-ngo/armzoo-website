About Us

ArmZoo NGO is an inclusive educational and therapeutic initiative in Armenia. We support children through nature-based learning, animal-assisted therapy, psychosocial support, and community programs.

Our mission is to create a safe, inclusive environment where children can heal, learn, and grow through nature and animal-assisted therapy.

Professional NGO Profile

Welcome to the official website of ArmZoo NGO. Thank you for visiting us.

ArmZoo is a newly established non-governmental organization focused on supporting children's development through educational programs, nature-based learning, and interaction with animals.

Our educational center is currently in the development and preparation stage and is not yet fully operational.

We are actively working on building our programs, environment, and future activities to create a safe, inclusive, and supportive space for children and the community.

On this website, you can follow our progress, activities, and future plans.
